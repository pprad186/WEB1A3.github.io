Temple Clean-ups (2016-2021) — Kathmandu, Nepal

For five years, I volunteered with classmates and teachers to clean two local temples in my school area. These annual clean-up events helped promote cleanliness, respect for cultural spaces, and community involvement.

This experience taught me leadership, discipline, teamwork, and the importance of contributing to shared cultural environments.

Campus Mural Painting (2017) — Kathmandu, Nepal

I collaborated with seven fellow schoolmates to paint the interior campus walls with beautiful murals including nature scenes, meadows, peacocks, and mountain landscapes.

This project aimed to inspire students and represent the importance of nature, culture, and creativity within the school community.

Orphanage Support & Donations (2016-2020) — Kathmandu, Nepal

For four years, I volunteered with five friends at a local orphanage where we donated:

  • Clothes
  • Toys
  • Storybooks
  • Basic necessities
  • Financial contributions

Spending time with the children and offering support strengthened my compassion and understanding of community care.
