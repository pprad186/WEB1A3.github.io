Transcript

Hello! My name is Prerana. I am an international student from Nepal studying Computer Science at Mount Royal University. I am currently in my fourth year of the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program. I enjoy drawing and painting in my free time, and I have included some of my artwork in the Art page of this website. And you'll probably notice when looking at the Resume page that I have been working non-stop the last four years, of course, within the legally allowed hours per week which is 24 hours per week at the moment for international students while I am in school and working at least 80 hours a WEEK with 2 to 3 jobs over every summer to afford living expenses here in Calgary and my expensive tuition which is three times more than domestic students. So I haven't had much time to draw recently, but I hope to get back to it soon. I hope you find my ePortfolio informative and engaging. Thank you for visiting!
