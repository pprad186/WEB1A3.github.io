My Artwork

I've enjoyed drawing and painting since I was young. These are some of my favorite pieces I've created over the years. I have used various mediums including pen, pencil, color pencils, oil crayons, and spray paint.

Reflection

I have been drawing and painting since I was five years old, and over the years, it has become a central part of my life.Starting with simple sketches as a child, I gradually explored different styles, techniques, and mediums, pencil and watercolors. Drawing helps me relax.This long-standing practice has not only helped me develop my artistic skills but has also taught me patience, attention to detail, and the value of creative expression.

I find that the problem-solving mindset I use in programming often inspires new artistic approaches, and conversely, my artistic thinking allows me to think more creatively about technical challenges.

Although I primarily use traditional mediums, I am interested in exploring digital art in the future. I hope to combining my passion for art with my computer science background, perhaps exploring digital illustration, animation, or interactive media. I aim to develop projects that blend technology and creativity, allowing me to grow both as an artist and as a technologist.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to discuss art techniques or share your own work!
